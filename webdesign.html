Sidebar

This is the sidebar where you can add extra information, links, or advertisements.
In web design, a sidebar is an essential layout component used to enhance navigation, provide additional content, or display advertisements. Positioned to the left or right of the main content area, sidebars often contain menus, links, or widgets that offer users easy access to other parts of the site. They improve user experience by keeping important elements visible as users scroll through content. Responsive design ensures that sidebars adapt well to different screen sizes, often collapsing into a drawer or menu on mobile devices, making them versatile in both desktop and mobile layouts.
